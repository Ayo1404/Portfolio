Projects & Experience

Bike Gearbox Design – Second Year

Developed a gearbox for an Electrically Assisted Pedal Cycle (EAPC), transmitting power from an electric motor to the rear wheel. Achieved a top 10% performance result.

My role focused on designing a reduction system and adapting motor-control code. A prototype failure during testing required me to lead a rapid redesign, implementing robust joints and revising the code. The final design maximised efficiency and durability, achieving strong test results. This project developed my skills in CAD, FEA simulation, prototyping, problem-solving under pressure, and teamwork.

Water Bottle Rocket Launcher – First Year

Designed and built a compressed-air powered launcher, integrating safety mechanisms and trajectory modelling.

Combined MATLAB simulations with physical testing to optimise pressure, water volume, and launch angle. Iterative improvements led to increased accuracy and stability. On test day, all rockets landed within 10m of the target, earning full marks. Strengthened my analytical approach, modelling skills, and attention to detail.

Engineering You’re Hired – University Challenge

Worked in a multidisciplinary team to design an implant-based biosensor to reduce infection rates in hip and knee replacements.

Tasked with creating a sustainable, biocompatible solution that minimises infection risks. My role was researching antimicrobial coatings and developing an inclusivity policy to ensure our solution was patient-centred. The final design projected up to a 30–50% reduction in revision surgeries. Gained experience in collaboration, research, and innovation in healthcare engineering.

Global Engineering Challenge

Collaborated with engineers from multiple disciplines to design transport infrastructure for a rural Cambodian village. Awarded the Dr. Trish Murray Professional Behaviours Team Award.

Our team identified flooding resilience as the primary challenge and proposed a raised laterite road with culverts and a compacted gravel surface. I contributed to analysis, decision matrices, and cost evaluations. This project developed my teamwork, problem-solving, and cross-cultural engineering awareness.
